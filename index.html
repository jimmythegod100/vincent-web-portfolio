About Me

Hi, I'm Vincent — I build clean, professional websites on Wix.

I'm a 16-year-old website builder focused on helping small businesses and creators get online with polished, mobile-friendly pages. I work primarily in Wix, where I handle layout, branding, contact forms, and connecting your social profiles so customers can find and reach you easily.

FAQ

Common questions

How old are you / how much experience do you have?
I'm 16 and newer to freelancing. I've built practice sites and helped with real webpage projects using Wix. I'm honest about what I know and what I'm still learning.
Do you only use Wix?
Yes — that's my main tool right now. It's great for small business sites, portfolios, and simple stores. I don't do custom-coded websites or complex app development.
What do I need to provide?
Your business name, text for each page, photos (or I can help pick placeholders), and any social media links. A logo idea helps — or I can create a simple one.
Will I own the website?
Yes. The site lives on your Wix account. You pay Wix directly for hosting/plan if needed, and you keep full access.
What can't you do?
Custom programming, large e-commerce builds, advanced SEO campaigns, or anything that needs a senior developer. I'll refer you elsewhere if it's beyond my level.
